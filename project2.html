Project 2: UX/UI Logo Design

About this Project

I conducted a UX study using Normans theory of the Three Levels of Processing: visceral (first impressions), behavioral (what the design encourages users to do), and reflective (interpretation over time). The goal was to evaluate which logo design best reflects the identity of the subject, in this case a computer science student who is also a bakery pastry lead and has a Palestinian heritage.
